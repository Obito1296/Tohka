Tohka Yatogami (夜刀神 十香 Yatogami Tohka) He is one of the main characters of the Date A Live series, he is the first spirit that appears in the story and the second of which Shido Itsuka seal their powers. After being saved from the AST, Tohka is transferred with the help of Ratatoskr to Raizen High School, where she becomes Shido's classmate. Inside Tohka resides a second personality that Shido gave the name of Tenka (天香?), Which only emerges when Tohka assumes her inverted form after falling into a deep state of despair.





Appearance

Tohka is a girl with dark purple hair and eyes that are deep purple on the top and a mixture of yellow and pale pink on the bottom half. In addition to having the white aloe in the center of his pupil for being a spirit. Shido described her as "An incredibly beautiful girl".


Upon her arrival on Earth, she wears a dress with a black, cream and pink color and a set of purple and cream silver armor that adorns her shoulders and waist. The dress is open at the back, and comprised of a black corset that exposes some of her breasts and a two-layered knee-length skirt that has a pink to white gradient. Her hair is tied back using a butterfly-shaped hair accessory and a pair of gloves that have cuff-like petals, both share the same color scheme as her suit, and she wears a pair of armored boots.


Upon entering Raizen High School, she adopts the uniform of that school, despite wearing a skirt similar to the dress she wore upon arrival.

Her cuteness has no limits, in the world ranking tohka yatogami occupies the 6th place of the most beautiful waifus.

Tohka is a highly offensive fighter who uses a sword as her primary weapon. Aside from this, Tohka also has a large amount of "mana" within her Spirit body, increasing her offensive and defensive abilities. You can place a powerful barrier around it, use energy blasts with your hands or fingers, and create shock waves with a simple arm movement.



His main weaponry is the Sandalphon, which takes on the appearance of a golden throne that doubles as the scabbard of his sword. Tohka's fighting style mainly consists of strong and fast physical attacks with "mana" locking in her sword and body, or a high energy attack with her sword.



The throne itself appears to be a kind of support machine. Depending on how Tohka uses it, the throne can be turned into a transport ship by placing it horizontally. By cutting the throne in half, you unlock the final form of his weapon; , a huge single-edged sword shaped like a zanbatō that dramatically increases the destructive power of its Angel.



The resistance of Tohka's astral dress can withstand even a core explosion, since as seen in the anime she can resist space waves with her astral dress and space waves are worse than nucleus explosions.



Even when without her spirit mana, Tohka has displayed three abilities that can be considered supernatural. The first is her incredible strength and speed, which, even when not enhanced by spirit mana, has proven to be far above that of an average human, breaking the record for girls at Raizen High School for a physical examination, while she was, in fact, doing her best to contain herself. The second is the ability to instinctively sense if she is in danger, which was demonstrated when she was able to sense that she was being watched by the DEM, but could not feel anything when Ratatoskr, who had no intention of hurting her, was looking at her. during his first battle with Ellen, he could instinctively sense that she was not an opponent he could resist.Finally, the third ability is an enhanced sense of smell, which he can use to easily locate or identify someone.

To describe the Tohka Spirit's ability in a few words, Tohka is a very offensive fighter who uses a sword as her primary weapon. Apart from this, Tohka also has large amount of "mana" in her spirit body that increases both offensive and defensive ability.

His main weaponry is the Sandalphon, which takes on the appearance of a golden throne that functions as the sheath of his sword. His combat style consists primarily of either physical attacks with "mana" coating on the sword and body, or a high energy attack (such as a laser beam), combined with his sword.

The throne itself is a kind of aid machine, depending on how you use it. It can be changed into a transport ship by placing her horizontally, or to unlock the final form of her weapon, Halvanhelev, by cutting the throne into two exact halves. Later in the series, after she got her power sealed by Shido, she manages to regain her power a bit from her during the Yoshino created crisis that threatens Shido.
